Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

TarotGo ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

TarotGo is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. All tarot readings, preferences, and data are stored locally on your device.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the app's performance and user experience. This data does not identify you personally and may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App crashes and performance metrics
  • Feature usage statistics

2. How We Use Your Information

The limited information we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve app functionality and performance
  • Fix technical issues and bugs
  • Understand how users interact with the app
  • Develop new features based on user needs

3. Data Storage and Security

All your tarot readings, card of the day history, preferences, and other personal data are stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to this data, and it is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

4. Third-Party Services

TarotGo does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your information to outside parties.

5. Children's Privacy

TarotGo is suitable for users of all ages. Since we do not collect personal information, we comply with children's privacy regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act).

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your information:

  • You can delete all app data by deleting the app from your device
  • You can manage your preferences within the app settings
  • No account creation or registration is required

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
